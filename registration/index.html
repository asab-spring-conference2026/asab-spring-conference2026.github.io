Abstract submission is open (see below), and closes on 1st February 2026.

Registration for the conference and workshop (including refreshments and social events) costs £75 for undergraduate/postgraduate/unwaged attendees, or £90 for all other attendees. Please go to our Eventbrite page to register. Registration closes on the 19th February 2026.

ASAB offers conference attendance grants for eligible conference attendees (where many postgraduates and other ECRs are likely to be eligible) - the deadline for applying for one of these is 1st February 2026. In addition to Conference Attendance Grants, ASAB also gives Accessibility and Childcare Grants to facilitate conference attendance. Details of all of these, including application links, are available at the main ASAB Grant site. Successful grant applications will be announced shortly after the deadline.
